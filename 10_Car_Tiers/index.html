  1. Not older than four years
    • Checked?
  2. At least 4mm of profile depth
    • Checked?
  3. All 4 tires are of the same model, age and have the same wear & tear
    • Checked?
  4. Tires are suitable for the current season
    • Checked?

Seasonal Tires

Tire Seasons
Type Summer season Winter season Notes
16.04. - 31.10. 1.11. - 15.04.
Summer tires ✔ ✖ Rubber has better traction and less attrition on hot days/streets
Winter tires ✔ ✔
  • Rubber has better traction on mud & snow and cold days
  • Has to have the M+S symbol
All-year tires ✔ ✔ Can be used all year round, but never provide optimal performance
